What we do

Electrical work for homes & small businesses

From a single dodgy power point to a full rewire or a new build fit-out — if it's got wires in it, Fred can sort it.

01

Rewires & New Wiring

Full and partial rewires for older Richmond homes — new cabling, points and circuits without tearing the place apart.

02

Switchboard Upgrades

Swap tired old fuse boards for a modern board with RCD protection, so your power stays on and your family stays safe.

03

Lighting & LED

Downlights, outdoor and security lighting, and swapping old fittings for efficient LEDs that cut your power bill.

04

Power Points & Faults

New outlets where you need them, plus fault-finding for tripping breakers and dead circuits — usually sorted first visit.

05

Heat Pumps & Appliances

Wiring for heat pumps, ovens, hot water cylinders and appliances — connected safely and to spec.

06

New Builds & Renos

Wiring your new build or renovation from plan to sign-off — working in with your builder and hitting your timeline.
